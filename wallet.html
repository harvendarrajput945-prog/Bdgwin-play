BDG Win Wallet Guide: Deposits & Withdrawals

Managing your funds safely is very important. Follow our step-by-step instructions to securely add money or withdraw your earnings from your BDG Win account.

How to Deposit Money into Your Wallet

To start playing, you need to add funds to your wallet. Use these simple steps:

  1. Step 1: Log in to Your Profile — Sign in to your account app or portal.
  2. Step 2: Open Wallet Section — Click on the "Wallet" button located in the navigation bar.
  3. Step 3: Select "Deposit" — On the next screen, tap the Deposit option.
  4. Step 4: Choose Payment Mode — Select from available options: UPI-QRPay, UPI-PayTM, Wake UP-APP, USDT, or ARPay.
  5. Step 5: Enter Amount — Type the exact amount you wish to deposit into your account.
  6. Step 6: Submit Request — Click the "Deposit" button at the bottom and finalize your payment transaction.

How to Withdraw Money from Your Wallet

When you win cash rewards, you can easily transfer them to your bank account:

  1. Step 1: Access Wallet — Securely complete your login and navigate to the Wallet section.
  2. Step 2: Select "Withdraw" — Click the Withdrawal tab on the main wallet screen.
  3. Step 3: Choose Payout Method — Select either Bank Card transfer or USDT mode. Make sure your active bank account details are linked correctly.
  4. Step 4: Enter Withdrawal Amount — Enter the specific amount you wish to cash out.
  5. Step 5: Confirm Request — Click the final "Withdraw" button to process your payment. Your funds will reflect in your account shortly.
Important Disclaimer: Always cross-check your bank card details or UPI addresses before finalizing any transactions. Incorrect details may lead to failed or misplaced payments.
