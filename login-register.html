BDG Win Registration & Login Guide

How to Create an Account on BDG Win Game

  1. Step 1: Click the Register button on the home page.
  2. Step 2: Enter the active mobile number you want to use.
  3. Step 3: Create a strong password of your choice.
  4. Step 4: Confirm the password you just entered.
  5. Step 5: Enter the Invitation Code if you have one (Optional).
  6. Step 6: Accept the privacy agreement and terms.
  7. Step 7: Click the register button to complete your registration.

How to Complete the BDG Win Login Process

  1. Step 1: Go to the login form.
  2. Step 2: Enter your registered phone number.
  3. Step 3: Enter your correct account password.
  4. Step 4: Check the "Remember Password" option so you don't have to enter it every time.
  5. Step 5: Click the login button to enter the dashboard.
